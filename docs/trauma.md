# Trauma & Burns

## Major Trauma (Adult)
- Royal Stoke University Hospital (UHNM) is the referral centre for **chest trauma and polytrauma**.

### Cardiothoracic
- Via referapatient (chest wall reconstruction).
- For urgent advice: call UHNM switchboard **#6136** and ask for Cardiothoracic Registrar on-call.

### Neurosurgery
- Via referapatient (QMC Nottingham).
- Neurosurgical registrar: **07812 275352**

## Burns (Predominantly Nottingham)
- SHO on call mobile: **07812 276431**
- Adult Clinic NCH: **0115 9691169 ext 76405**
- Paeds Clinic QMC: **82388 / 80364**
- Burns Outreach team: **07812275381**
- Send pictures via Outlook if needed (see Photography guidance) for IG compliance.

### Paediatric burns (NCH recommendations)
- Referrals/advice directed to plastics doctors on call.
- Weekend/weeknight referrals may be triaged on weekday morning; dressings clinic Mon–Fri 8–4pm (predominantly nurse-led).

## Hand referrals to RDH
- Contact Hand Fellow via RDH switchboard.
- Mobile: **07788 388457**
- Send a copy of the Cas sheet to **dhft.hands@nhs.net**

## Polytrauma (Adult)
- Call UHNM switchboard **#6136** and ask to page the Trauma Team Leader (TTL).

### Regional Trauma Desk (WMAS)
- Hospital line (call first): **01384215697**
- Emergency line (if above fails): **01384215695**

## Major Trauma (Paediatric)
- Birmingham Children’s Hospital is the local Paediatric MTC.
- All children needing transfer should be referred via **KIDS** (do not phone ED/specialty area/RTD):
  - **KIDS Tel:** **0300 200 1100**
- KIDS coordinates and provides TTL advice; if time-critical, local team may still need to transfer.
